About Shark Buffalo

Who the hell is Shark Buffalo, that mysterious Ã¼bergroup from a long-forgotten Missoula music scene of yore? More importantly, what the hell is a shark buffalo ? Do you picture a buffalo's body with a shark's head, like some kind of aquatic centaur? Or do you prefer to envision an elasmobranch fish characterized by a cartilaginous skeleton fused with the head and torso of a bearded bovinae?

We digress.

Shark Buffalo is comprised of an unholy musical trinity: Keys, Drums and Trumpet. Cove Jasmin, codename CavemanJazz, forms the pineal gland and cloven hooves of this hideous monster, stomping and clomping in a rhythmic fashion, propping this ancient juggernaut upright and propelling it forward with unstoppable momentum. Emmet Ore, codename Eore, embodies the torso, digestive organs, and nervous system of the behemoth. Perpetually hungry, he is an ectothermic killer who eternally meanders through the murky musical abyss in search of the faintest electrochemical signal of a musical idea to latch onto and rip to shreds without remorse. Nathan Crawford, codename "Rockbottom", personifies the musical brain, lungs, and horns of the colossus, alternating between sweet, angelic melodies and guttural, satanic bellows as it laments its freakish, horrible existence in a cruel and uncaring world, utterly alone.
